Cloudiness

Clouds form when the water vapor in the air condenses into visible water droplets or ice crystals. In theory, there will be more clouds in the prescence of water. This would allude to higher cloudiness in coastal areas with latitude having no effect. We observe this in the plot above where there is no discernible trend.
